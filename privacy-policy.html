Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Zikr Counter handles your information when you use our mobile application.

We take your privacy seriously. This app is designed to not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers or any third parties beyond what is necessary for advertising functionality.

Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection

Zikr Counter does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Device identifiers (other than those used by AdMob for advertising)
  • Location data
  • IP address (other than standard ad serving)
  • Photos, contacts, or files
  • Biometric or health data

Local Device Storage Only

The app stores the following data locally on your device only (never sent to any server):

  • Counter values for each zikr
  • App theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Daily reminder notification settings
  • Usage streak information
  • Vibration toggle preference

All local data is stored using the device's application documents directory. This data can be permanently deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device advertising ID (Google Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Approximate location (non-precise, derived from IP address)
  • App usage data for ad relevance

For more information, see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy & Terms

Google Play Services / Apple App Store

The app is distributed through the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. These platforms may collect standard analytics and crash data as part of their normal operation. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Device Permissions

Permission Purpose
Notifications (Android/iOS) To send daily reminder notifications
Internet (Android) Required by Google AdMob to display ads

No other permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage, etc.) are requested or used.

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell or rent your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes
  • Transfer your data across borders for processing
  • Use any analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)

The only data sharing that occurs is through Google AdMob as described above.

Data Security

Since the app stores all data locally on your device, the security of that data depends on your device's built-in security features. We recommend using your device's screen lock and keeping your operating system updated.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and/or on the app store listing. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

  • Right to know what data is collected
  • Right to delete your data (uninstall the app to remove all local data)
  • Right to opt out of personalized advertising (through your device's ad privacy settings)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or similar jurisdictions, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data

  • Withdraw consent at any time
